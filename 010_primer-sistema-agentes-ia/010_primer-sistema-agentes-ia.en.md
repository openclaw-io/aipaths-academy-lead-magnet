## Why download this roadmap?

Using AI every day does not mean your business has an AI system.

Many people open ChatGPT, explain the context, ask for a task, fix the result and start from zero again the next day. That helps, but it does not scale.

This roadmap shows how to move from scattered tasks to a first AI agent system with a clear structure: which process to choose, which tool category to use, what context the agent needs and how to measure whether the system is actually helping.

## What is included

- **The 3 levels of AI maturity**: chat and copilots, automations, and agent systems.
- **A 7-step roadmap** to design your first system before jumping into tools.
- **Criteria to choose the first use case**: frequent, clear, reviewable and low-risk.
- **How to define input, output and success criteria** before automating.
- **When to add memory, tools and automation** without amplifying a messy process.
- **A complete competitor analysis example** turned into a weekly system.

## Who is it for?

For entrepreneurs, creators, consultants and small teams that already use AI, but still feel that every task depends too much on them.

You do not need to have a multi-agent system installed. The guide is designed to help you choose the right first process before building.

## What you will be able to decide after reading

1. Which process in your business should be improved first with agents.
2. Which type of system or tool category makes sense for that use case.
3. What concrete first step to take without improvising.

The goal is not to learn more tool names. The goal is to design a more repeatable, clearer and more useful way of working for your business.
